Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

[Agiltym SAS] ("we", "our", "us") operates the Targetym mobile application (hereinafter "the App") and the Targetym AI web service accessible at dashboard.targetym.ai (hereinafter "the Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service, as well as the choices available to you regarding this data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Data we collect

1.1 Data provided by the user

  • Professional email address
  • First and last name
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Job title and department within the company
  • Social security number (NIR) — only for payroll features
  • Emergency contact information (optional)

1.2 Data generated by usage

  • Leave requests, absences, sanctions
  • Performance reviews, OKRs, training records
  • HR documents, certificates, contracts
  • Internal messages and exchanges

1.3 Technical data collected automatically

  • Device identifier (for push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging and Apple Push Notification service)
  • Device type and operating system (Android / iOS)
  • IP address (server logs, kept for 30 days)
  • Date and time of login
  • Pages viewed and actions performed (internal analytics)

1.4 Mobile app permissions

The Targetym mobile app requests the following permissions:

PermissionWhy
CameraTake a profile photo or scan an HR document

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your phone settings. Some features may then become unavailable.

2. Use of your data

We use your data to:

  • Provide the Service: authentication, profile management, access to HR features
  • Communicate: notifications of requests, approvals, important alerts
  • Improve the Service: analyze anonymous usage to optimize the experience
  • Security: detect fraudulent access, prevent abuse
  • Legal compliance: meet our legal and regulatory obligations

We never sell your data to third parties.

3. Data sharing

3.1 Within your organization

HR managers, line managers, and authorized administrators of your company, according to the roles defined by your administrator.

3.2 Technical sub-processors

We use third-party providers to host and operate the Service:

  • Hosting: Amazon Web Services (AWS), [Europe / Africa] region
  • Push notifications: Google Firebase Cloud Messaging (Android), Apple Push Notification service (iOS)
  • Transactional email: Resend
  • Artificial intelligence: Anthropic (Claude API) for AI features
  • Business identity verification: Dun & Bradstreet (D-U-N-S)

All these providers are contractually required to protect your data.

3.3 Legal obligations

We may disclose your data if required by law, in particular upon judicial request.

4. Data retention

  • Active account data: as long as your account is active
  • Connection logs: 30 days
  • Data after account deletion: 30 days then complete purge
  • Legal HR documents: according to the mandatory legal duration in your country (e.g., 5 years for payslips in France, 10 years in Senegal)

5. Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • TLS encryption for all network communications
  • At-rest encryption of sensitive data in the database
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) available
  • Regular security audits
  • Restricted access: only authorized persons from your organization can access your data

6. Your rights

In accordance with the GDPR and applicable local laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: obtain a copy of your data
  • Right of rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: request the deletion of your account
  • Right to data portability: receive your data in a reusable format
  • Right to object: refuse certain processing
  • Right to withdraw consent: at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: [support@targetym.com]

We respond within a maximum of 30 days.

7. Cookies

The Targetym web service uses essential cookies to:

  • Maintain your logged-in session
  • Remember your preferences (language, theme)
  • Secure authentication

The mobile app does not use cookies (uses Capacitor's secure local storage).

8. Children

The Service is not intended for persons under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you discover that a child has provided us with data, contact us so that it can be deleted.

9. International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence. When this is the case, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place (standard contractual clauses, certifications).

10. Changes
